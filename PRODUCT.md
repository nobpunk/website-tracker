# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Active crypto traders, analysts, and investors who need a fast, real-time interface to monitor price movements, track their watchlists, and get instant AI-driven technical analysis and market insights without leaving their dashboard.

## Product Purpose
A high-fidelity, real-time crypto market tracking dashboard that integrates live prices (via Binance WebSocket) and an interactive TradingView chart with a secure, backend-driven AI market assistant. It provides automated technical analysis, support/resistance detection, trend summaries, and significant price warnings, secured by Twitter/X OAuth.

## Brand Personality
- **Terminal-Native:** Clean, data-dense, and highly functional.
- **Professional & Precise:** Monospace formatting for numeric data, zero fluff.
- **Intelligent:** Subtle, integrated AI assistance that feels like an expert co-pilot rather than a chatty bot.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS marketing sites with bright purple-to-blue gradients, rounded bubble cards, and low-contrast grey text.
- Over-decorated, slow-loading dashboards with gratuitous entry animations and generic loading spinners.
- Clickbait financial advice or AI-generated promises of "guaranteed profit."

## Design Principles
1. **Data is the Interface:** Information density is a feature. Visual decorations are minimized in favor of clear grids, high-contrast typography, and real-time updates.
2. **Contextual Intelligence:** AI analysis is displayed alongside the relevant market data and charts, keeping the workflow unified.
3. **Frictionless Real-Time:** Price feeds and charts load instantly and update with sub-second latency. Loading states use skeletons matching the layout.
4. **Absolute Transparency:** Clear disclaimers that AI outputs are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict WCAG AA contrast ratios (especially for green/red price indicators, which must be clearly distinguishable).
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to disable real-time ticker animations or transitions.
- Full keyboard navigation for key dashboard actions.
